üèóÔ∏è Technical Architecture

Multi-Chain Architecture

Flexibility and Experimentation
Polkadot's architecture reflects its philosophy of community sovereignty through parachains that can have their own logic, consensus, and governance while benefiting from shared security.

Key Components:

  • Parachains: Sovereign blockchains with their own rules
  • Relay Chain: Shared security and coordination
  • XCM: Native cross-chain communication
  • OpenGov: Decentralized on-chain governance
  • BABE/GRANDPA: Optimized hybrid consensus
Technical Philosophy: Prioritizes flexibility and experimentation over pure optimization. Each parachain is an innovation laboratory.

Architectural Advantages:

Native Interoperability: Parachains communicate directly without external bridges.

Heterogeneous Scalability: Different parachains can optimize for specific use cases.

Shared Security: All parachains benefit from Relay Chain security.

Three-Chain Architecture

Specialization and Efficiency
Avalanche uses three specialized chains (X-Chain, C-Chain, P-Chain) optimized for specific functions, reflecting its philosophy of efficiency and superior performance.

Key Components:

  • X-Chain: Native asset exchange
  • C-Chain: Smart contracts (EVM compatible)
  • P-Chain: Validator and subnet coordination
  • Avalanche Consensus: Sub-second finality
  • Subnets: Custom blockchains
Technical Philosophy: Prioritizes optimization and efficiency. Each chain is specialized to maximize overall system performance.

Architectural Advantages:

Superior Speed: Transaction finality in under 1 second.

EVM Compatibility: Easy migration from Ethereum.

Linear Scalability: Performance increases with more validators.
